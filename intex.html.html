How It Works

Intelligence Behind Every Recommendation

Our engine blends multiple ML techniques to surface movies that match not just your genre — but your cinematic DNA.

1

Content Analysis

Movies are vectorised using TF-IDF on plot, genre, director, and cast into high-dimensional space.

2

Collaborative Filtering

SVD matrix factorisation identifies hidden user taste patterns from 100,000+ ratings.

3

Cosine Similarity Ranking

Candidates ranked by angle distance in embedding space — closer angle means stronger match.

4

Hybrid Ensemble

Content + collaborative scores blended with tuned weights, yielding 83% precision overall.
